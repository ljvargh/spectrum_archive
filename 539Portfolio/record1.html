A Closer Look at at the Timeline Records

While exploring these records, you may notice that some records predate the founding of the Spectrum Center, while others do not directly mention the Center. These records are included to give historical context leading up to the Spectrum Center's founding, and to highlight the campus climate during various parts of the Center's history.

Deborah Bacon Memo

Year: 1953
This is the first page of a memorandum written by Deborah Bacon, who at the time was the University's Dean of Women, to then-President Harlan Hatcher. The memo speaks of two women students "behaving in a most conspicuous manner" because they were openly affectionate with each other.

Women's Liberation Newsletter

Year: 1970
There were many activist groups and publications in Ann Arbor in the 1970s, and many had ties to the University. This is the cover of self-piublished Women's Liberation Newsletter from the era.

Gay Liberation Front Ad

Year: 1971
The Gay Liberation Front was a student and community group, and in this article they publicize information about their organization and encourage people to attend meetings.

Lesbian and Gay Male Directory

Year: 1977
Since our founding in 1971, the Spectrum Center has gone through many name changes. What has not changed, however, is our commitment to providing information and resources for LGBTQ+ students.

Michigan Daily Article: Gay Community Active

Year: 1981
This article from the Michigan Daily reports on how members of the LGBTQ+ community planned events and activities to create a more vibrant, active community.

Michigan Daily Article: Student Protest Coverage

Year: 1984
This article from the Michigan Daily covers students to express their displeasure with University President Shapiro, and to call for policy change to address discrimination against LGBTQ+ people.

Michigan Daily Article: Kiss-In

Year: 1993
This Michigan Daily article covering the first "Queer Kiss-In" at the University of Michigan also discusses the reactions and political implications of the event.

Michigan Daily Article: 3rd Annual Kiss-In

Year: 1995
This Michigan Daily article covers a Queer Kiss-In event organized by a campus group called the Queer Unity Project to "celebrate our right to express affection for those we love."

Michigan Daily Article: National Coming Out Week

Year: 1995
This is the first page of the Michigan Daily's coverage of activities for National Coming Out Week at the University of Michigan.

Michigan Daily Article: Kiss-In Celebration

Year: 1996
This Michigan Daily article covers the Queer Kiss-In event, reporting both on the event and reactions to it, as well as the broader political climate for LGBTQ+ people on campus.
